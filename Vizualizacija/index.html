LeBron James Career Stats

LeBron James has played 21 seasons for the Cavaliers, Lakers, and Heat. He has averaged 27.1 points, 7.5 rebounds, and 7.4 assists in 1,492 regular-season games.

He has won the Rookie of the Year award, 4 MVP awards, 4 Finals MVP awards, and 4 NBA championships.

He was selected to play in 19 All-Star games.
